01 — About

Equal parts rigor and curiosity.

Statistician by training, political scientist by instinct, data scientist by trade — and a firm believer that the best models are the ones people actually understand.

🧠 What I do

On Fannie Mae's Collateral Products & Modeling team, I lead research on property valuation modernization and appraisal bias. My work blends machine learning with policy thinking to build models that are accurate, fair, and actually usable — and I mentor junior data scientists along the way.

🚀 What I'm learning

Pursuing an MPS in AI Management at Georgetown, digging into how organizations deploy AI responsibly and at scale. My take: the future of AI isn't just better models — it's better leadership around those models.

🌎 Off the clock

Raised in Seattle, so the coffee snobbery is genetic. I'm a devoted foodie and Beli power user, always logging the best restaurants, bars, and bakeries wherever I land. I travel every chance I get — Mexico City has my whole heart — and like any good data scientist, I'm endlessly curious. That curiosity is exactly why this website exists.
